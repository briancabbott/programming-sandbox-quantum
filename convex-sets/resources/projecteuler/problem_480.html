Define P(w) as the position of the word w.
Define W(p) as the word in position p.
We can see that P(w) and W(p) are inverses: P(W(p)) = p and W(P(w)) = w.

Examples:

  • W(10) = aaaaaacdee
  • P(aaaaaacdee) = 10
  • W(115246685191495243) = euler
  • P(euler) = 115246685191495243
