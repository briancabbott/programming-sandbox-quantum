xn = (1248n mod 32323) - 16161
yn = (8421n mod 30103) - 15051
Pn = {(x1, y1), (x2, y2), ..., (xn, yn)}

Let C(n) be the number of triangles whose vertices are in Pn which contain the origin in the interior.

Examples:
C(8) = 20
C(600) = 8950634
C(40 000) = 2666610948988

Find C(2 000 000).
